Admissions
for M.Tech, M.S and Ph.D

M.Tech, MS and Ph.D in Computer Science and Engineering for academic session starting in July 2017.
  • Application window for admissions in academic session starting in July 2017 is from Mar 28, 2017 till Apr 21, 2016. Outside this window no applications will be entertained.
  • The application process is online. Please visit this URL to access the application portal.
  • Check the weblink for general information. Check the Dean of Administrative Affairs weblink for instructions on the application process and requirements.
  • For reference letter submission -- referee Online Portal must be used. Referees can submit their recommendations through "Referee Online Portal" (only) using login credentials provided through auto-generated email. The last date of submitting online Referee's feedback is April 27, 2017 (23:59 Hrs)
  • Candidates will be short listed and intimated by end of April if they will be called for onsite exam and interview.
  • All candidates have to appear for onsite exams, and there will be no Direct admission.
  • Tentative dates for onsite written tests and interviews are May 12-14, 2017.
  • Logistics of accommodation at student hostels during the interview dates will be announced after the short listing.
  • The syllabus for the written test is here .
  • On the first day of onsite exams, a written test based on the given syllabus will take place in the morning. It will be followed by a 3 hour long programming test. The programming test will be in C language.
  • Based on the results of the written and programming tests, candidates will be short listed.
  • Shortlisted candidates for M. Tech. will be given an advanced written test. Shortlisted candidates for MS by research and PhD will be interviewed instead.
  • Selected Candidates list will be made available a few days after the onsite exams. The selected candidates must be ready to join in the second half of July (exact dates will be specified later).
